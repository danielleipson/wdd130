Rafting

The Colorado River is world-renown for its legendary whitewater rapids; some of the largest whitewater in North America froths deep within the canyons of Utah and Arizona. These gargantuan and spectacularly clad canyons and landscapes through which the Colorado River plunges are no less famous.

Camping

Camping around the Colorado River is popular among ATVers and boaters. The area includes hundreds of miles of beautiful shoreline beaches along the Colorado River as well as vast mountainous terrain. There are plenty of campgrounds to choose from.
